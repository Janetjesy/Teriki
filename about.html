Our Story

Where timeless elegance meets modern inclusivity. Discover the passion, purpose, and people behind Teriki.

Born from a Desire for Authentic Beauty

Teriki was founded on a simple yet profound belief: makeup should not mask who you are, but rather celebrate the individual beauty you already possess. For too long, the beauty industry dictated narrow standards of perfection. We wanted to change that narrative.

In 2024, our founders, frustrated by the lack of genuinely inclusive shade ranges and clean, high-performance formulas, set out to create a brand that empowers women of all ages, skin tones, and backgrounds to feel absolutely radiant. From our humble beginnings mixing pigments in a small studio to our global community today, our mission has never wavered.

Inclusive Elegance

We meticulously formulate our shades to ensure everyone finds their perfect match, embracing the full spectrum of beauty.

Clean & Cruelty-Free

Our products are formulated without harsh chemicals, parabens, or sulfates. We love our furry friends and are 100% cruelty-free.

Empowering Community

Teriki is more than makeup; it's a supportive community where confidence is continuously cultivated and celebrated.
