1. Information We Collect

When you shop with Teriki Beauty or interact with our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal details – your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you place an order or create an account.
  • Payment information – billing details required to complete your purchase. We do not store full card details; payments are processed securely via trusted third-party providers.
  • Usage data – how you navigate our website, including pages visited, time spent, and referring sources, to help us improve your experience.
  • Communications – messages, emails, or enquiries you send to our team.
2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders and send order confirmations.
  • Provide customer support and respond to your enquiries.
  • Send you updates, promotions, and beauty tips — only if you have opted in.
  • Improve our products, website experience, and marketing.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations.
3. Sharing Your Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Delivery & logistics partners to fulfil your orders.
  • Payment processors to handle secure transactions.
  • Analytics providers (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand site performance.
  • Legal authorities if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
4. Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us remember your cart, preferences, and login status. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling them may affect some site functionality.

5. Data Security

We take the security of your personal data seriously. We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption (SSL/TLS), secure servers, and restricted internal access to your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at jaycijay35@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for individuals aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with their data, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.
